At Family Crest, we understand that businesses face unpredictable risks, including fire hazards and marine transit losses. Our Fire and Marine Insurance Plans are designed to provide comprehensive protection against potential financial setbacks caused by fire damage, natural calamities, or transportation risks, ensuring the safety of your assets and goods.

Fire Insurance

Our Fire Insurance Policy safeguards businesses, factories, warehouses, offices, and homes against loss or damage caused by fire, explosions, lightning, riots, storms, floods, and other natural or man-made disasters. The policy covers building structures, machinery, stock, furniture, and other valuable assets, helping businesses recover quickly after an unexpected event.

With optional add-ons like loss of profit coverage, third-party liability, and debris removal costs, we ensure that your business operations remain financially secure.

Marine Insurance

Our Marine Insurance Policy provides financial protection against loss or damage to goods, cargo, or ships during transportation by sea, air, or land. Whether you are an importer, exporter, manufacturer, or logistics provider, our policy covers risks like theft, piracy, storms, vessel collisions, or accidental damage.

We offer different types of marine insurance, including Hull Insurance (for ships and vessels) and Cargo Insurance (for goods in transit), ensuring complete coverage for your business.
